About InfoTech Managed Solutions

Company Background

InfoTech Managed Solutions (Pty) Ltd, founded in February 2014 by Mr. Siyabonga Nkambule and operational since July 2014, was established to address a clear market need for Information Technology, energy-saving solutions, industrial supplies, and construction services.

As a 100% black-owned enterprise, we blend innovative technologies with practical approaches to deliver cost-effective energy solutions and maintain a competitive edge in industrial supply. We offer the comprehensive resources of a large corporation with the flexibility of a medium-sized firm, ensuring excellence and exceeding client expectations. Our foundation filled a crucial economic gap in South Africa, promoting youth-owned business involvement.

A core commitment is our active involvement in women empowerment and community development initiatives, which have significantly contributed to our growth.

Our Business Description

InfoTech Managed Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic Supply and Consulting Company. Our primary focus areas, though not limited to them, include information technology, industrial supplies, the implementation of energy-saving strategies, and engagement in community development projects. While categorized as a small business enterprise, the company is structured for potential expansion and growth as it achieves continued success.

Vision, Mission & Values

Our core principles guide every aspect of our operations, ensuring excellence and integrity in all we do.
